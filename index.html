Mission statement

The 40Yr Virgil exists to pursue footballing excellence within the strict confines of whatever the servers allow. We honour the badge, we trust the process, and we hold the unshakeable belief that this is our season — a belief renewed automatically at the start of every season.

We are committed to total football, total loyalty, and a strict rotation policy of never rotating Tupci off CAM.

The name

Why "The 40Yr Virgil"? Some legends are written. Ours is still loading.

The founders know. The founders aren't telling — yet. When the story drops, it drops right below this paragraph.
